Introduction

Welcome to AlgoLearn! We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data, ensuring that your experience using our app is safe, secure, and respects your privacy.

By using AlgoLearn, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

Data Collection

At AlgoLearn, we do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This means we do not ask for or store data such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiable information.

Our primary goal is to provide you with a learning platform that is both effective and secure. As such, we do not require any personal data for you to use our app. Your experience is fully anonymous, allowing you to learn without concerns about data privacy.

We do not track your activities outside the app, nor do we monitor your interactions with other applications or services. Our commitment to user privacy means that you can engage with our educational content without worrying about how your data is being used or shared.

Non-Personal Information

Although we do not collect personal information, we may gather non-personal usage data to enhance the performance and functionality of our app. This data may include information on how you interact with the app, such as the features you use, the time spent on various activities, and other similar metrics.

This usage data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you as an individual. It helps us understand user behavior and preferences, enabling us to continually improve the app's design and user experience. We may use tools and analytics services to assist with this data collection, but no personal information will be shared or collected.

Such data might include the type of device you are using, the operating system version, and basic app interaction metrics. This allows us to optimize performance and ensure a smooth experience for all users. However, rest assured that none of this data can be traced back to you personally.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

AlgoLearn may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences. However, we do not use cookies to track personal information.

Cookies can enable us to provide certain functionalities, such as remembering your preferences for future visits or analyzing how users engage with our app. This analysis can help us make informed decisions about how to improve the app for all users.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that declining cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the app.

Additionally, you can clear cookies from your browser history periodically, ensuring that no tracking data is stored on your device longer than necessary. We recommend reviewing your browser’s privacy settings to manage your cookie preferences effectively.

We do not share any data with third-party services. AlgoLearn operates independently, and we are committed to maintaining your privacy. In the event that we integrate third-party services in the future, we will update this policy to reflect those changes and ensure you are fully informed about what data may be collected and how it will be used.

We encourage users to be cautious when accessing external links or services through our app. We cannot be responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Our commitment is to keep your data secure, and we will not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

We are committed to regularly reviewing our security measures and adapting to new technologies to maintain a high standard of protection for our users.

If you suspect that your data has been compromised, please reach out to us immediately so we can take appropriate action to investigate and resolve the issue.
